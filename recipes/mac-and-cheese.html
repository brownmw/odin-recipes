Alton Brown's Mac & Cheese

Description

This deliciously gooey, yet crispy macaroni cheese is the perfect meal for a chilly evening when you feel the need for a little pick me up. Baked to perfection with a light breadcrumb crust, this meal is bound to go down a treat with everyone. Serves 6.

Ingredients

Pasta and "Sauce"
  • 230g macaroni
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 1 Tbsp english mustard
  • 710mL milk
  • 1 finely diced medium onion
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 large egg
  • 255g grated mature cheddar
  • salt
  • pepper
Breadcrumb Topping
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 85g panko breadcrumbs
  • 85g grated mature cheddar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 170Â°
  2. Add salt to a large pan along with boiling water and place on high heat. When at a rolling boil add the pasta and cook until al dente (around 8 minutes). Drain when cooked.
  3. While the pasta is boiling, melt the butter in a medium sized pan over a medium heat then add the flour. Mix to combine and cook for 5 minutes (keep it moving throughout).
  4. Stir in the milk, onion, bay leaf, mustard and paprika. Simmer for 10 minutes as the sauce thickens then remove the bay leaf.
  5. Temper the egg by placing a small glass bowl on a tea towel so it doesn't easily move. Whisk the egg until homogenous then with the whisk in your dominant hand and the ladel in the other, slowly pour a steady stream of the sauce whilst continuously whisking. You want to warm the egg without scrambling it. Once a few ladels of sauce have been incorporated and the egg and sauce mix are around the same temperature as the rest of the sauce. Pour the egg mixture in to the main sauce.
  6. Stir the cheese and mix until melted, then season with salt and pepper.
  7. Fold the cooked macaroni in to the sauce then pour in a suitably sized casserole/glass dish.
  8. Add the remaining cheese over the top of the pasta.
  9. Melt the butter in a saute or frying pan and add the breadcrumbs. Mix until evenly coated.
  10. Top the macaroni with the breadcrumbs.
  11. Bake for 30 minutes and rest for 5 minutes before serving.
